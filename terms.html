Digital Banking Compliance

1.0 Data Encryption

All transaction data transmitted through Trust Bank (TB) nodes is encrypted using 256-bit AES protocols. All access is logged via hardware node ID.

2.0 Multi-Currency Policy

Accounts holding multi-currency balances are subject to fixed exchange rates provided by the TB Global Ledger. Cross-currency transfers include a 1% processing fee.

3.0 Anti-Money Laundering (AML)

TB reserves the right to hold any disbursement exceeding $5,000 for manual audit. Identity verification (KYC) is mandatory for all high-limit accounts.
